How to make the best cinnamon rolls

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 3/4 cups milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter
